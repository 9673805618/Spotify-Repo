Name : Shreya Ghoshal
DOB : 12 March 1984
bio : Shreya Ghoshal is an Indian singer and television personality. One of the highest-paid and most well-established playback singers of Hindi cinema, she has received four National Film Awards, four Kerala State Film Awards, two Tamil Nadu State Film Awards, seven Filmfare Awards and ten Filmfare Awards South. She has recorded songs for films and albums in various Indian languages and has established herself as one of the leading playback singers of Indian cinema.
